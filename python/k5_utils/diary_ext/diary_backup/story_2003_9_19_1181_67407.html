Report from inside Isabel (Diaries)

By llimllib
Fri Sep 19th, 2003 at 01:18:01 AM EST

I know you're all excited to hear that I am, indeed, inside Isabel right now.

The winds are at 16 mph consistently, gusting to 24 mph, according to weather.com. It's pretty damn breezy outside. I sat on the porch outside my friends' house watching the storm and the light-to-heavy rain come down. It's nice and cool outside, the breeze feels nice. Also, I got to watch drunk people walk in the rain from the party down the street. That's always amusing.

Isabel, Isabel, why don't you pack more punch?
