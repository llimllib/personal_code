You know those soda cap contests? (Diaries)

By llimllib
Tue Jun 22nd, 2004 at 08:37:22 PM EST

I think that, instead of saying "Sorry, play again" if you don't win a soda cap prize, the cap should compliment you. You know, like, "That cream you're using really cleared up your rash" or "are you a model?". CheeseburgerBrown's latest outbursts of random compliments reminded me of this thought of mine, which occurred to me a while ago. Cheers for bringing it to the World's Wide Web, CBB.

Other disjointed thoughts:

  •  

    I'm kinda pissed that Spain dropped out of Euro 2004. Why can't they ever manage to put together a decent tournament run? Also unfortunate, the English managed to get out of the group stage. Hopefully the Portugese take them to school. I guess I'll root for the Danes, since nobody that I really like made it through the group stages (unless Latvia gets through, in which case, go Latvia!)

    Also, I found a local Indian place that has satellite coverage of all the games, so maybe I'll go watch some.

  •  

    The Red Sox are still going to win the AL East. I wish they'd won the series against the Giants, but oh well.

  •  

    Anybody think Martina Navritalova (sp?) can win her next game at Wimbledon?

  •  

    I think I'm going to start applying for shit jobs tomorrow. Supermarkets and video rental stores, here I come!

  •
