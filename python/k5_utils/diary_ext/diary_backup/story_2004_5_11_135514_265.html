my girl is obsessed with sex (Diaries)

By llimllib
Tue May 11th, 2004 at 01:55:14 PM EST

Up until a few weeks ago, I had been kissing a girl, R, for some time. We didn't have relationship or any such deal, but we had a tacit agreement that we liked to hang out with each other. She was a very attractive brunette, and we had some good times.

Unfortunately, there were a couple of problems. She likes to take pills - adderol, adrenaline, and sleeping pills. She thought that the solution for her current mood was to take a pill in the other direction. Feeling a little up? take a downer. A little down? take an upper! Also, she was nearly always drunk; while this was good for my sex life, it was getting a little annoying.

The Pursuit

During the same period of time, I had been chasing, on and off, a girl named J. J is a tall, thin, blonde, which is just my type. She is in the same dance troupe as R, and a girl named A. In fact, she looks quite a bit similar to A; make of that what you will. That will only make sense if you've read the previous diary.

At one point, I made a visit to J's room late at night. My fraternity and her sorority had a party that night, and J and I had been dancing and flirting for a good portion of that time. When I went to visit some friends late, after the party, they said they knew where her room was. We made a visit over there, primarily because one of the guys I visited had been hooking up with J's friend. Things went well, we were flirting some more, until I had to go to the bathroom. When I came back to the room, her current boyfriend was in the room, standing in the position I had been in when I left, holding Jenna.

I got up to wash my face
When I come back to bed,
someone's taking my place
Whoa cecilia...
In a funny aside, her boyfriend at the time and I almost got each other kicked out of school in this very building when we were freshman (that story is a good one, and for another diary). Now here we were, both seniors within months of graduation, pursuing the same freshman.

The capture

Fast forward a couple of weeks. I'm still hooking up with R, but really looking to get out of that. There's another party at J's house, this time for greek week. R is not greek, so I can be there uninterrupted. I meet J inside after this party, and she suggests that we go hang out at my suite. Naturally, I'm interested, as are a couple other people there, and we all head towards my suite. I manage to walk alongside J, and determine with no subtley whatsoever that she is no longer with my rival - Score! But it would not be so easy.

Who comes up to the suite, but both R and K (mentioned here), making my task more difficult. Up in the suite, everyone was playing beer pong, or playing drinking jenga, or just chilling, but I had a mission. I will leave out details, but eventually, J and I were headed out on a walk towards the track. I'll let you know that high jump mats are very comfortable.

The Conclusion

What amazes me about J and her freshman friends is how sex-obsessed they are. I've hooked up with my share of girls at college, but not one of them compares to any one of these girls. J, herself, is ready pretty much anytime for some fun. "Play with meee!", she whines when I'm not attentive enough. J is at least chill compared to her roommate and one of her friends that comes into her room to hang out; those two will bang anything that moves (or sometimes might not).

Last night, J and I didn't hang out, because I was playing poker and had a headache afterwards. Here's what she IMs me this morning:

J: omg i was soo drunk last night and all we wanted to do was watch porn lol
J: it was soo funny
llimllib: haha
J: and i wake up today and there is soo much on my comp..i was soo confused
I mean, it's interesting and all, but I'm not sure what to do about this girl. Oh well, graduating on saturday, we'll see what happens after.
