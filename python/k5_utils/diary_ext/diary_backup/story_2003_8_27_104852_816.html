Girl go home (Diaries)

By llimllib
Wed Aug 27th, 2003 at 10:48:52 AM EST

I had coffee with A last night. I don't think we'll ever have a friendly conversation again.

I went to A's room at about 8 to pick her up, and we went to the cafe about 15 minutes away from my school. I drove, because even though she has a better car I can't stand not being the driver. Everything was small talk, something we have never had a problem with. We got to the coffeeshop only to discover that there was a 6 dollar cover charge because a jazz band was set to play. Since we'd already driven there, and there's no alternatives in our rural area, we decided to pay up. Fortunately, she volunteered her own cover, and we sat down.

Side note: even though she's fairly well off and I'm pretty broke, why do I still feel like shit letting her pay for herself when we're out? Oh well. Anyway.

I had a latte, which was good. Lattes always remind me of my time in Spain, becuase that's the closest you can get here to what is a cafe there. So, we sat down and started listening to the band. They had a keyboardist, drummer, bassist, and saxophonist (can I shorten that? saxer? saxist?). The keyboardist sang on most of the songs, while the rest were instrumental, but the vocals were too low and I couldn't hear a damn word he sang. When he wasn't singing, though, I liked what they were doing. He had a really nice solo.

For the first half hour, we sat at our table, continuing our small talk and listening to the band. Once I had gotten my fill of the band, I got down to the real business, that of telling her what I felt. Basically, I told her that I wanted to be her only guy. She said "I feel like there's no middle ground", and that's exactly what I was trying to say. We clearly can't be friends when she wants me to be there for her all the time, but she's never there for me.

After what seemed like an uncomfortable eternity, and significant prodding, she told me that she wasn't ready to break up with her boyfriend for me. This was not unexpected. What was unexpected was that I felt free and easy now that the task was over with. In fact, I would almost say that I enjoyed it, overall. I feel bad for her, because she does care about me in some fucked up way, but if she can't keep from fucking with my head, then we can't be friends.

So, we're done, and I feel good. Now, between today and saturday, I'm going to hook up with one of the 3 girls I'm pretty sure that I can hook up with. Yeah college :)
