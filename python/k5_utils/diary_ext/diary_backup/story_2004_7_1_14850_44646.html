Montreal Jazz festival (Diaries)

By llimllib
Thu Jul 1st, 2004 at 02:08:50 PM EST

I'm headed to Montreal this weekend to play in the Montreal Jazz ultimate frisbee tournament. Hopefully, I should also catch some of the jazz festival itself. I wish that I could afford Chick Corea tickets, but alas, I cannot. Hopefully I'll be able to catch some of the free shows.

I would ask if any Montrealers wanted to get a beer, but I don't know what my schedule is going to be like at all. So, instead, I'll just ask if anybody else is going to the festival?

Inside stuff be unrelated, yo. Music, books, GPL, and those f*$%ing redsox.

  •  

    I'm picking up with some random team from University of Guelph. What a strange name for a school. I hope they don't all suck, but I'm glad I found a team to pick up on.

  •  

    I went back to the DMV and got myself a Connecticut license. It looks spiffy, with a painting of a lighthouse on Long Island Sound in the background. My picture, unsurprisingly, sucks.

  •  

    Chick Corea rocks, as do Eric Dolphy and Roy Hargrove. They're my newest borrows from the library, along with Pat Metheny. I also took out Chaos, by James Gleick, and the autobiography of Gandhi. They both seem good, judging from the first 50 pages.

  •  

    Can I use the card face images from a GPL poker program in my program if it's non-GPL? Is that considered "linking", or can I just include the images as GPL and use them in my program? All this licensing shit confuses me. I emailed the author of the program asking for an exemption, and he said that I can only use them if they're GPLed.

    I don't even know how I'm going to license my program, but I don't want to be tied to GPL if I can avoid it.

  •  

    Goddamn Red Sox. CATCH THE BALL, PEOPLE! YOU'RE PRO BASEBALL PLAYERS AND LITTLE SCHOOLGIRLS CATCH BETTER THAN YOU!!!

  •  

    phew. I feel better now.
