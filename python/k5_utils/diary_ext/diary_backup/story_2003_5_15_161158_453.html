Mr. Abooey, trolling Red Sox fans is not nice (Diaries)

By llimllib
Thu May 15th, 2003 at 04:11:58 PM EST

This has been two days in a row. The members of the K5RSA (K5 Red Sox Alliance) demand, on penalty of severe taunting, that you cease and desist. And the Indians suck.

In other news, moving out sucks, The Matrix deux might suck (but I sure don't want to hear your thoughts about it), and trolls definitely suck. Read on.

I'm in a ranting mood, and this seems the place for it:
  • This is THE year for the Red Sox. It just is. If you don't think so, then you're wrong. Nomah is taking the Sox all the way.
  • Moving out sucks. I'm not moving until next week, but I've been helping friends move all day. They're at our storage locker right now, and I will continue moving shit when they return to pick up the last load.
  • As predicted, there are too many links to thoughts about the Matrix. Also as predicted, I don't care, you probably don't care, and the people who find their thoughts so important that they need to tell us about The Matrix really shouldn't care.
  • That's the second time in this diary I've used repetition. Repetition. Maybe I should stop. stop.
  • Is it just me, or does anyone else think that Cux is the least clever troll around? At least the other ones can occasionally come up with something amusing. Cux, could you pick it up, please?
  • Now that my venom has been spat, some happier thoughts. How would you write an anti-blog? what would that consist of? what is the opposite of blog?
